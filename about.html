about Eveneum Solutions

We are a specialist recruitment consultancy focused on finance, accounting, BPO and KPO hiring. Based in Ahmedabad, we partner with companies across India to build reliable, skilled teams.

who we are

Eveneum Solutions is more than a recruitment agency. We focus on the complexities of finance and accounting hiring while supporting BPO/KPO and operations roles. Our team combines hands-on recruiting experience with a strong network built through referrals and long-term partnerships.

our approach

We emphasize quality over quantity: targeted sourcing, structured screening, and close collaboration with hiring managers. Our aim is to save time, minimize hiring mistakes, and deliver candidates who add measurable value to your teams.
