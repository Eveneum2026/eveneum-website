pricing (placement fees)

Placement fee is calculated on gross annual CTC. Any applicable taxes are extra. Payment due within 15–30 days of candidate joining.

CTC band Placement fee (% of gross annual CTC) Payment timeline
Up to ₹10 lakhs 8.33% Within 15–30 days of joining
₹10–20 lakhs 9.99% Within 15–30 days of joining
Above ₹20 lakhs 11.99% Within 15–30 days of joining
Temp / Part-time Depends on role & duration As agreed

guarantee

For experienced hires: 60-day replacement or as per the agreed term. For freshers: 90-day one-time free replacement. Eveneum covers sourcing, reference checks and basic background checks as part of the fee.
