Process

How We Work

Our recruitment process is designed to minimize hiring risk and maximize long-term fit

01

Discovery & Briefing

We meet with hiring managers to understand role requirements, team dynamics, reporting structure, and cultural expectations.

02

Targeted Sourcing

Using professional networks, referrals, LinkedIn outreach, and internal databases, we identify candidates who match the role profile.

03

Structured Screening

Candidates are evaluated for technical skills, communication ability, cultural fit, and career motivations through structured interviews.

04

Candidate Presentation

Shortlisted candidates are presented with detailed profiles, assessment notes, and salary expectations for your review.

05

Interview Coordination

We coordinate interview schedules, provide candidate preparation support, and gather feedback to keep the process moving efficiently.

06

Offer & Onboarding

Once a candidate is selected, we assist with offer negotiation, acceptance, and smooth transition into your organization.
